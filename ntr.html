N. T. Rama Rao Jr.


Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr. (born 20 May 1983)

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao, also known as Jr NTR or Tarak, is an Indian actor, singer, and television host who works in Telugu Cinema. He is mostly known for his single take acting, dialogue delivery and dancing without rehearsals.
